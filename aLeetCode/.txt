# 🧠 What you should master with vector

Before moving to maps/sets:

* `push_back()`
* `pop_back()`
* `size()`
* `resize()`
* `sort()`
* `reverse()`
* `erase()`
* Iteration using:

  * index
  * iterator
  * range-based loop
